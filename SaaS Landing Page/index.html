Common questions

Flowra connects via OAuth or API key — no code required. Choose from 200+ pre-built integrations in the connector library, authenticate once, and your app is ready to use in any workflow. We never store your credentials in plaintext.
Yes. Any tool that exposes a REST or webhook endpoint can be connected using Flowra's HTTP connector. On Scale, you can also publish private integrations that are only visible to your team.
All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted with AES-256. Flowra is SOC 2 Type II certified and undergoes regular third-party penetration testing. We process workflow data ephemerally — we don't store the contents of your records beyond what's needed to execute a run.
Flowra has three roles: Admin, Builder, and Runner. Admins control team access and billing. Builders create and edit workflows. Runners can trigger and monitor workflows but can't modify them. Role assignment is per-workspace on Growth and Scale.
Failed runs are logged with a full error trace and highlighted in your run history. You can configure retry logic per step — up to 5 attempts with exponential backoff. Email and Slack alerts are available on all paid plans so your team is notified immediately.
